    Syntax IQ is an innovative Quiz game designed to test and enhance your coding abilities. Developed with Unity, the game features a series of challenging levels where players solve syntax-based Quiz and master programming concepts through fun and interactive gameplay. Whether you're a beginner looking to improve your understanding of code or an expert trying to sharpen your skills, Syntax IQ has something for everyone.
